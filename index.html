About The Poll
What Is DVPoll?
Anonymous Polling Platform
DVPoll is a secure and anonymous online polling platform designed for Christian communities. It fosters honest conversations about faith, personal struggles, and spiritual growth without fear of being identified.
Every vote is completely anonymous. We do not collect your name, social media details, or any personally identifiable information. All responses are compiled as collective community insights.
Key Features
· Real-time live vote counts
· Countdown timer for fairness
· One-tap sharing
· Installable PWA — works offline
· Poll, Announcement & Lecture screens.
Developed by God's servant, Rev. Don Victor, PhD, a soul winner, trainer, and skilled software developer, with a mission to present Christianity and Christian education in a way that resonates globally and fosters broad biblical acceptance.
Privacy Promise
DVPoll collects anonymous usage data to improve the user experience and never your identity.
